About US

PakWheels.com is an online marketplace for buying and selling cars in Pakistan. It was founded in 2003 by Muhammad Hanif Bhatti and is based in Lahore. Early history PakWheels started as a community for car enthusiasts to share their passion for cars In 2005, it became Pakistan's first online classified website for vehicles In 2007, Raza Saeed and Suneel Munj bought PakWheels from Bhatti Growth and success PakWheels has become Pakistan's top automotive portal It has a forum for automotive discussions, a blog, and a review section for cars and bikes PakWheels has helped millions of Pakistanis buy and sell cars It has an 80% market share of the automobiles classified ads market It has created new revenue streams and hopes to provide a one-stop solution for automotive transactions Other activities PakWheels also hosts the PakWheels Auto Show, which raises funds for charities The show has attracted many celebrities, government officials, and car enthusiasts About Us - Pakwheels Founded in 2003, PakWheels.com has become Pakistan's #1 automotive portal.
